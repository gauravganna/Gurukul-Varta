2nd Senate Meeting
Gaurav Ganna
Contributor
28 June,2018


The 2nd Senate meeting of IIT Goa was convened on 28th June, 2018 in its temporary Campus in GEC Farmagudi. The meeting was conducted under the chairmanship of Prof. B. K. Mishra, Director, IIT Goa and was attended by the nominated members of Senate as well as heads of the concerned departments from IIT Bombay. 31 members, special invitees, and invitees including student’s representative participated in the meeting in person and 5 members participated through video conferencing.

Members of the Senate discussed various agenda items related to Academic function of the Institute. To begin with, Registrar-in-charge presented the action taken report based on the minutes on the 1st senate meeting. Following that the Senate discussed and gave its approval to some important agenda items such as introduction of M. Tech Programs, establishing IIT Goa Centre of Excellence on ‘Particles, Colloids & Interfaces’ and Centre for Rural Development and Technology. The Academic performance of the students in the past semesters and PhD admission report were also presented and discussed in the meeting. The meeting concluded with some valuable suggestions from various attendees.
